Be out of comfort zone every day! Push yourself! Pressure yourself etc. These kind of things you hear everyday on youtube and social media!
Well being constantly out of your comfort zone and under pressure is a complete bullshit. It makes you anxious. It is bad for your health.

You want to have a fullfilled life that makes you motivated, happy and gives you meaning.

Increasing your productivity, effecienty and leveling up is absolutely essential to a fullfilled and happy life. Being successful and financially free is great. But you DO NOT achieve it through puting yourself out of comfort zone every day.

There is positive pressure and negative pressure.

The thing is if you want to grow, in any area of life you have to CONSIOUSLY DECIDE to push yourself towards a SPECIFIED GOAL and then REWARD youself if you hit that goal. That process is "fully consious". You DECIDED to do it. YOU set that GOAL. You pushed yourself and put youself under positive pressure without making youself anxious and having constant stress wether to hit the goal or not.

Being constantly under pressure and anxious is bad and negative. Do not allow anyone, including yourself to do that to you. Resist accepting new jobs or responsibilities if you think you can not handle it. Calm yourself down when under negative stress and tell yourself i DO NOT ALLOW this stress to make me bitter. Instead, Set yourself CONSIOUS - Realistic Goals, Commit to hit them and then REWARDS yourself everytime you hit those.

That is the right way of growth.

Enjoy Yourself.
