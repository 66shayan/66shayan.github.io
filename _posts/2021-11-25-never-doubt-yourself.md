Life happens to you. You will have setbacks wether you want them or not. People push you back and try to diminish you and your self confidence. You will lose jobs... etc.

All kinds of painfull things will happen to you and let me tell you, Life will give you enough reasons to completely doubt yourself and become miserable.

It is completely up to you to push back and believe in yourself enough to not let any situation make you doubt yourself and your abilities. Never doubt yourself.
If you are the kind of person that try to become better everyday, if you know you have a good knowledge about yourself and what you do and you consistently try to take feedback and fix your
mistakes then there is no room to self-doubt. It is DANGEROUS. It kills your progress. It makes you fearful and it does not let you to take advantage of your opportunities.

Faith, my friend. Faith in you and faith in what god have in pocket for you is the single biggest reason that makes you invincible and unshakable! And i don't know where that faith comes from
but i know it comes from within you and YOU need to find it inside yourself.

Have faith!
