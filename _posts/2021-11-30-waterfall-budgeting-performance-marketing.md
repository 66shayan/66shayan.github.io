When you do a lot of Performance Marketing and Paid Advertising for your business, you need to know how you are going to dedicate your budget to different channels. 

How much for Google Ads, How much for Display Ads, How much for Social Media etc. 

 

You do it often based on the potential of those channels, CPA (Cost Per Acquisition) and ROAS (Return On Ad Spend). Waterfall budgeting model is one of the best models when it comes to Digital Media Budget Allocation and Performance Marketing. 

 

Basically, You will spend your Budget on your best channel (In terms of CPA and ROAS) until it works. Even if it requires all the budget. Only when the channel is full and you can no longer get new users with logical CPA you will start spending on the second channel.
